Trip planning

Planning tips

Good travel planning should reduce friction without making the trip feel over-managed. These tips keep the basics covered.

Packing

  • Pack layers instead of one heavy outfit.
  • Keep a small essentials pouch for documents, chargers, and medication.
  • Leave room in your bag so you are not starting the trip overpacked.

Timing

  • Book major transport early, then leave smaller decisions flexible.
  • Build in extra time for transitions between stops.
  • Do not overload every day with activities.

Budget

  • Separate fixed costs from flexible spending.
  • Keep emergency money aside from the main travel budget.
  • Prioritize spending on the parts of the trip that affect experience most.
