Travel support

Support services

This page gathers the practical services that often matter once a trip is underway, from transport and food to gear, cleaning, and local help.

Service categories and hours

  1. Transportation
    • Airport shuttle desk — daily, 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM
    • Regional bus service — daily, 6:00 AM to 10:30 PM
    • Harbour ferry office — Monday to Sunday, 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM
  2. Food and supplies
    • Waterfront grocery — daily, 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM
    • Late-night convenience store — daily, 24 hours
    • Outdoor market hall — Friday to Sunday, 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM
  3. Safety, health, and maintenance
    • Walk-in clinic — Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM
    • Pharmacy — daily, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM
    • Bike and gear repair — Tuesday to Sunday, 9:30 AM to 6:00 PM
  4. Cleaning and trip support
    • Laundry service — daily, 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM
    • Luggage storage desk — daily, 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM
    • Visitor information centre — daily, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM

Why this matters

Good support information lowers friction. When transport, food, health, and repair options are easy to find, a place feels easier to move through and easier to enjoy.

The categories here are grouped the way a traveller would usually need them: first to get around, then to eat, then to handle issues if something goes wrong.
